Security & Governance Standards

We treat data security and compliance as primary design goals rather than secondary concerns. Our architectures enforce strict Row-Level Security (RLS) policies on database layers, utilize TLS 1.3 encryption transit streams, and offer dedicated private virtual private clouds (VPCs) on AWS and Google Cloud.

Additionally, we integrate custom token tracking logs and caching pools to guarantee operational auditability, complete observability, and cost control for enterprise deployments.

Our Engineering Principles

1. Validation & Grounding

AI outputs must be verified against predefined JSON schemas before consumption. We implement RAG grounding rules to prevent model hallucinations.

2. Operational ROI

We build workflows targeting manual operations bottlenecks, aiming for measurable reductions in cycle time (typically 60-80% savings).

3. Observability & Logging

Every decision path, database query, and LLM call is recorded in structured telemetry logs for auditability and compliance.

4. Support & Maintenance

All systems are covered under a 24-hour critical alert SLA, including prompt fine-tuning and routine agent telemetry optimization.
