Logistics AI

Automate Outbound Dispatching & Logistics Planning

Manual delivery scheduling and dispatcher-driver communications are operational bottlenecks. Originyx designs custom AI solutions that plan delivery itineraries, optimize routing, and track fleet data in real-time.

Why Introduce AI in Logistics?

Logistics dispatchers spend hours translating raw customer cargo requests, matching cargo parameters (weight, dimensions, target destination) to truck availability, and manually calculating routes.

By integrating route reasoning models with existing databases (Supabase, PostgreSQL), we automate the matching process. Driver communications are automatically parsed and structured operational alerts are generated to ensure route safety.
