HunterOS

Autonomous AI SDR Platform for Lead Prospecting & Personalized Outreach 🤖✉s

HunterOS is an autonomous AI Sales Development Representative designed to automate the entire outbound B2B lead generation loop. Operating as a private agentic network, it identifies target companies, scrapes public directories, extracts deep organizational pain points, and drafts hyper-personalized, context-driven outreach copy.

Status: Active Development
Tech: Python / Multi-Agent / Scraping
Audience: B2B Teams & Agencies

The Problem

B2B prospecting is heavily manual and inefficient. Sales Development Representatives (SDRs) spend hours finding lists, searching company pages, figuring out organization charts, and drafting emails. Sourcing and personalization do not scale: reps either send high-volume generic email blasts that get flagged as spam, or manually research every account, yielding a low outbound speed.

Additionally, B2B data decays quickly. Contact numbers and email databases bought off list sellers are often inaccurate. Sales pipelines stall because teams cannot efficiently locate qualified prospects or detect corporate paint points at scale.

Our Solution

HunterOS acts as an autonomous sales intelligence companion that runs outbound prospecting loops 24/7. It integrates state-of-the-art web scrapers and reasoning models to mine leads, evaluate corporate directories, detect business challenges, and customize copy.

HunterOS empowers sales teams to:

  • Automatically locate targeted leads matching Ideal Customer Profiles (ICP)
  • Crawl company sites, blog posts, and press releases for context
  • Extract structural bottlenecks and software integration gaps
  • Draft personalized cold email copy containing authentic references
  • Orchestrate specialized agents to coordinate pipeline stages

Architecture & Pipeline Workflow

HunterOS uses a structured multi-agent loop to maintain accuracy and prevent LLM drift. Rather than asking a single model to perform the entire operation, tasks are partitioned across three nodes:

  1. The Lead Prospector Node: Connects with open B2B databases and directory search scripts to isolate companies matching specific filters (revenue range, team sizes, and industry tags).
  2. The Corporate Ingestion Researcher: Runs headless crawling actions (using Jina Reader/Playwright) to extract text content from target homepages, about pages, and blog feeds, building a local context profile.
  3. The Personalized Copywriter Node: Evaluates the extracted text files to identify challenges (e.g. lack of chat support, slow website speed, broken links) and drafts customized pitch emails linking features directly to those gaps.

This pipeline records intermediate files and logs decision paths, enabling human managers to review prompts and audit copy before scheduling outreach.

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes HunterOS different from other email scraping software?

Generic scraping applications only harvest contact rows and insert them into basic templates. HunterOS reads company copy, detects actual operational pain points, and structures custom copy tailored to those challenges.

How does the agent pipeline prevent model hallucination?

All data inputs are parsed against strict schemas (like Pydantic targets) at every stage. The Copywriter node is strictly constrained by source metadata and cannot reference features or facts not present in company documents.

Is human verification supported?

Yes. HunterOS records drafts in a local database dashboard. Outbound emails are only queued and sent after review and approval by your team members.
