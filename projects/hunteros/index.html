HunterOS

Autonomous AI SDR Platform for Lead Prospecting & Personalized Outreach 🤖✉️

HunterOS is an autonomous AI Sales Development Representative designed to automate the entire outbound B2B lead generation loop. Operating as a private agentic network, it identifies target companies, scrapes public directories, extracts deep organizational pain points, and drafts hyper-personalized, context-driven outreach copy.

Status: Active Development
Tech: Python / Multi-Agent / Scraping
Audience: B2B Teams & Agencies
GitHub

The Problem

B2B prospecting is time-consuming and inefficient. Sales Development Representatives (SDRs) spend hours finding companies, reviewing websites, identifying pain points, and drafting cold outreach copy. Mass email blast templates result in low engagement and high spam rates, whereas manually tailoring every outbound message is slow and does not scale. Sales teams need a solution that balances scale with high personalization.

Our Solution

HunterOS acts as an autonomous sales intelligence companion that runs B2B prospecting loops 24/7. It identifies lead pools, researches company websites, detects potential pain points, and drafts hyper-personalized cold outreach messages.

HunterOS empowers sales teams to:

  • Automatically find niche leads matching B2B target profiles
  • Analyze website content and ingest public company data
  • Extract potential friction points and operational pain points
  • Generate personalized, contextual cold outreach copies
  • Orchestrate multiple specialized agents for parallel workflows
  • Accelerate sales pipelines without spamming
