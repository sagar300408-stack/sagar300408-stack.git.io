HunterOS Engage

AI-Powered Customer Engagement Platform 🤖💬

HunterOS Engage is an AI-powered customer engagement platform that manages the entire customer journey, from the first message to the final conversion. Natively operating across WhatsApp and web channels, it qualifies leads, schedules meetings and site visits, automates follow-ups, remembers customer preferences, and keeps every interaction organized inside a unified business dashboard.

Status: Production
Tech: Python / WhatsApp AI / AI Agents
Audience: Sales Teams & Service Businesses
GitHub

The Problem

Businesses lose customers every day, not because they have a poor product, but because conversations are forgotten, follow-ups never happen, appointments are missed, and valuable leads disappear before becoming customers.

  • Customer inquiries are scattered across WhatsApp and phone calls.
  • Follow-ups depend on human memory and are often forgotten.
  • Leads become cold because businesses respond too late.
  • Meeting and site visit scheduling takes unnecessary time.
  • Sales teams have no complete view of customer conversations.
  • Business owners struggle to track conversions, appointments, and sales performance.

Business Context

Designed by Sagar M at Originyx, HunterOS Engage was conceived out of a crucial market gap: small and medium enterprises (SMEs) were drowning in manual conversational overhead. By leveraging robust frameworks like FastAPI and scalable persistence with PostgreSQL, Originyx aimed to bridge the gap between high-level CRM tools and accessible chat interfaces like WhatsApp. The objective was to create an autonomous pipeline capable of qualifying leads exactly when they are most engaged.

Our Solution

HunterOS Engage transforms customer communication into an intelligent sales process by acting as your AI-powered customer engagement team, available 24/7. It doesn't replace your sales team—it empowers them by organizing conversations, eliminating manual follow-ups, and ensuring every customer receives timely attention. Engineered at Originyx, this platform guarantees uninterrupted engagement.

With HunterOS Engage, businesses can:

  • AI responds instantly to customer inquiries.
  • Every lead is automatically captured and qualified.
  • Meetings and site visits are scheduled without manual coordination.
  • Customer conversations are securely remembered for future interactions.
  • Automated follow-ups ensure no opportunity is forgotten.
  • A centralized dashboard provides complete visibility into leads, appointments, conversations, and business performance.

The Workflow

The workflow begins the moment a customer sends a message on WhatsApp or the Web Widget. The AI agent instantly processes the natural language input, matching it against business context. If the user indicates intent, the agent autonomously triggers the lead qualification protocol. Following qualification, the agent seamlessly invokes the scheduling API to offer immediate calendar slots, successfully converting a passive inquiry into an actionable appointment within minutes.

Technical Architecture

HunterOS Engage uses a unified cognitive framework architected by Sagar M. The system operates on scalable infrastructure, blending real-time messaging with asynchronous task queues. The platform operates on these primary modules:

  1. Omnichannel Ingestion Agent (FastAPI): Natively connects to WhatsApp (via Twilio/Meta Business API) and Web Widgets. Built on FastAPI for high throughput, it manages incoming webhooks in real-time, matching messages to unified lead profiles.
  2. Contextual Memory Hub (PostgreSQL + Supabase): Keeps structured records of customer choices, past interactions, and requirements. By leveraging PostgreSQL via Supabase, it ensures the AI rapidly references prior details on subsequent follow-ups.
  3. Autonomous Scheduling Node: Connects with booking platforms (Google Calendar/Cal.com) through secure OAuth flows to automatically propose open slots, coordinate, and reserve tours, meetings, or calls.

Intermediate lead logs and details are securely synced to the frontend dashboard built with React and TailwindCSS, giving business owners full operational visibility.

Implementation & Challenges

During the development phase at Originyx, one of the primary technical hurdles was managing context windows for conversational AI during lengthy customer interactions. Retaining historical context without overflowing LLM token limits required designing a smart summarization and retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) pipeline. Furthermore, ensuring a seamless connection between the WhatsApp Meta API and our internal FastAPI infrastructure necessitated robust webhook security and asynchronous processing using Celery and Redis to prevent timeouts during peak traffic.
