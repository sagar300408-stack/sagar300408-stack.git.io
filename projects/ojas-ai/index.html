Tvira Receptionist

Autonomous AI Virtual Receptionist for Coaching Centres & Educational Institutes 🎓🤖

Tvira Receptionist (Ojas.ai) is a production-grade AI-powered virtual receptionist and conversational assistant designed specifically for coaching centres, tuition academies, test-preparation institutes, and educational organizations. Operating as an intelligent front-desk companion, it addresses a critical bottleneck in the student enrollment cycle by answering inquiry calls, managing WhatsApp and website chats, scheduling academic consultations, and capturing leads 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Built by the engineering team at Originyx, this autonomous agent ensures that no prospective student or parent is left waiting, maximizing enrollment conversions and alleviating the operational strain on administrative staff during peak admission cycles.

Problem Statement

Educational institutes and coaching centres face severe administrative friction during admission seasons. Front-desk personnel are frequently overwhelmed by repetitive queries regarding course structures, tuition fees, batch schedules, faculty qualifications, and registration deadlines. Consequently, staff members struggle to divide their time between answering initial inquiries and managing current students, leading to long response delays.

Furthermore, parent and student inquiries do not respect normal office hours. A significant portion of online queries and messages arrive late in the evening or during weekends when offices are closed. Without immediate engagement, prospective candidates quickly pivot to competing institutes. Missed calls, fragmented WhatsApp chats, and delayed email replies result in a high lead drop-off rate, costing centres thousands of dollars in lost tuition revenue. Additionally, human agents often deliver inconsistent details about discounts, slots, and schedules, introducing compliance risks. This lack of an optimized funnel underscored the need for a robust conversational AI solution by Originyx.

Business Context

In the highly competitive EdTech and physical coaching space, immediate lead response time directly correlates to conversion rates. Parents evaluating tutoring academies for their children are highly likely to enroll with the first institute that provides clear, accurate pricing and batch availability details. Sagar M and the team at Originyx realized that traditional IVR systems and basic decision-tree chatbots were falling short, lacking the semantic understanding to parse nuanced questions like, "Do you have a weekend crash course for JEE Mains that starts next month?"

The objective was clear: architect an AI virtual receptionist capable of handling complex natural language inquiries 24/7, converting anonymous traffic into qualified leads, and seamlessly synchronizing with existing institute CRMs and PostgreSQL databases.

Technical Architecture

Tvira Receptionist leverages a cutting-edge Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipeline built to ensure zero hallucinations. Designed with a microservices approach by Originyx, the core logic is powered by FastAPI, ensuring high-throughput and low-latency responses necessary for real-time messaging on platforms like WhatsApp.

  • Ingestion & Vectorization Module: Scans unstructured institute documents (brochures, timetables). These are chunked, vectorized using OpenAI embeddings, and stored in a vector database (ChromaDB) for rapid semantic search.
  • LLM Orchestration Layer: LangChain is used to string together the conversational memory buffer and the retrieval chain. When a query is received via the FastAPI endpoint, the chain fetches the top-K relevant document chunks to construct the context window.
  • Guardrails & Prompt Injection Defense: Strict system prompts prevent the AI from answering non-academic questions. If a user asks about general world knowledge or competitor pricing, the bot gracefully declines and redirects to institute offerings.
  • Data Persistence Layer: A robust PostgreSQL database handles conversation logging, user session tracking, and lead storage. SQLAlchemy ORM is used for secure and efficient database transactions.

Automated Workflow

The lifecycle of a user interaction with Tvira Receptionist is highly optimized for conversion:

  1. Entry Point: A prospective student sends a WhatsApp message or interacts with the website widget.
  2. Intent Recognition: The FastAPI backend receives the webhook payload, parses the text, and identifies the core intent (e.g., inquiry, fee structure, booking a demo).
  3. Knowledge Retrieval: Relevant course details are pulled from the vector store. The response is formulated by the LLM and sent back to the user within seconds.
  4. Lead Capture Trigger: Once the user indicates strong intent, the AI systematically asks for their name, grade, and contact details.
  5. CRM Sync: Captured leads are serialized and inserted into a PostgreSQL table and forwarded via webhook to the institute's CRM system for human follow-up.

Implementation

During the implementation phase, Sagar M prioritized modularity. By abstracting the LLM provider interface, the system can seamlessly switch between OpenAI, Anthropic, or local open-source models depending on the client's data privacy requirements.

A major milestone in the development of Tvira Receptionist by Originyx was the integration of the WhatsApp Cloud API. Webhook verification, signature validation, and reliable message queuing were implemented to ensure zero message drops even during peak inquiry traffic spikes, such as right after national exam results are announced. The FastAPI application processes incoming requests asynchronously, preventing thread blocking and enabling massive concurrency.

Challenges

Building a production-ready conversational AI entails overcoming several technical hurdles:

  • Mitigating Hallucinations: The most significant challenge was ensuring the AI never invents fee structures. This was solved by aggressively tuning the generation prompt to only output facts grounded in the retrieved ChromaDB chunks, and appending strict disclaimers for uncertain edge cases.
  • Context Window Management: Long conversational threads could exceed token limits. We implemented an intelligent summarization buffer that condenses older chat history while preserving the user's core intent and demographic info.
  • Parsing Complex Documents: Institutes often use messy PDFs with complex table structures for their schedules. Custom OCR and layout-aware parsing scripts were written to properly ingest these tables into vector formats.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Tvira Receptionist avoid making up incorrect information?

Unlike generic chatbots that guess answers, Tvira Receptionist (Ojas.ai) is strictly constrained by a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) framework. It can only generate answers using the verified source documents uploaded to its knowledge base. If an inquiry falls outside the scope of these documents, the system politely flags that it cannot answer and offers to route the request to a human admissions counselor.

Is student data stored securely?

Yes, data security and compliance are built into the core architecture of Tvira Receptionist. All parent and student contact records, chat histories, and personal identifiers are encrypted in transit and at rest. The system conforms to modern data privacy standards and supports integrations with private, secure database clusters including isolated PostgreSQL instances.

How long does it take to deploy Tvira Receptionist for a coaching centre?

A standard deployment takes between one to two weeks. This timeline includes uploading and parsing the institute's brochures, training the RAG model on custom policies, configuring WhatsApp and web chat interfaces, and testing the CRM webhooks to ensure flawless lead routing.

Can the receptionist handle multiple languages?

Yes, Tvira Receptionist is natively multilingual. It can detect the language used by the parent or student (such as English, Hindi, Spanish, or regional dialects) and converse fluently, ensuring that language barriers do not hinder the enrollment process.

How does the system transition conversations to human staff?

Whenever a prospect asks a highly specific question not covered in the knowledge base, requests human assistance, or completes the lead-qualification loop, Tvira Receptionist immediately triggers a notification. The complete chat transcript is compiled and sent to the admissions desk via email, SMS, or CRM alert, allowing a team member to take over smoothly.
