Tvira Receptionist

Autonomous AI Virtual Receptionist for Coaching Centres & Educational Institutes 🎓🤖

Tvira Receptionist (Ojas.ai) is a production-grade AI-powered virtual receptionist and conversational assistant designed specifically for coaching centres, tuition academies, test-preparation institutes, and educational organizations. Operating as an intelligent front-desk companion, it addresses a critical bottleneck in the student enrollment cycle by answering inquiry calls, managing WhatsApp and website chats, scheduling academic consultations, and capturing leads 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Built by the engineering team at Originyx, this autonomous agent ensures that no prospective student or parent is left waiting, maximizing enrollment conversions and alleviating the operational strain on administrative staff during peak admission cycles.

The Problem

Educational institutes and coaching centres face severe administrative friction during admission seasons. Front-desk personnel are frequently overwhelmed by repetitive queries regarding course structures, tuition fees, batch schedules, faculty qualifications, and registration deadlines. Consequently, staff members struggle to divide their time between answering initial inquiries and managing current students, leading to long response delays.

Furthermore, parent and student inquiries do not respect normal office hours. A significant portion of online queries and messages arrive late in the evening or during weekends when offices are closed. Without immediate engagement, prospective candidates quickly pivot to competing institutes. Missed calls, fragmented WhatsApp chats, and delayed email replies result in a high lead drop-off rate, costing centres thousands of dollars in lost tuition revenue. Additionally, human agents often deliver inconsistent details about discounts, slots, and schedules, introducing compliance risks.

Our Solution

Tvira Receptionist (Ojas.ai) operates as an autonomous, multi-channel front-desk assistant that handles student and parent interactions instantly. Trained on verified institutional data, it delivers accurate, approved answers to complex questions, captures lead profiles, and integrates with backend databases.

Tvira Receptionist (Ojas.ai) empowers educational teams to:

  • Provide 24/7 instant replies to prospective students and parents on all communication channels.
  • Maintain zero-hallucination standards by constraining the conversational agent to official syllabus and fee documents.
  • Capture qualified candidate details (exam categories, target scores, contact info) and auto-forward them to administrative systems.
  • Reduce operational support workloads by handling up to 80% of repetitive incoming front-desk FAQs.
  • Automate counseling bookings, allowing parents to schedule virtual or physical center tours on the spot.

Architecture & Multi-Agent Retrieval Loop

Tvira Receptionist (Ojas.ai) utilizes a structured conversational pipeline designed by Originyx to maintain strict factual adherence. The system uses a modular retrieval architecture to isolate reasoning and reference lookup:

  1. Ingestion & Vectorization Module: Scans and processes unstructured institution documents (brochures, class timetables, fee tables, and course FAQs). These are converted into high-density semantic embeddings and indexed in a secure database.
  2. Conversational Routing Node: Evaluates the incoming user query. If a student asks about specific schedules, discounts, or fee structures, the router triggers a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) query to pull the exact context from the verified knowledge base.
  3. Factual Verification Guardrail: Filters the retrieved knowledge chunks and processes them through safety templates, formatting the text into a natural, polite response while preventing the model from hallucinating or discussing unauthorized topics.
  4. Lead Generation Pipeline: Dynamically identifies when a conversation shifts to high enrollment intent. The node collects parent/student contact details and pushes this structured payload directly to administrative databases and CRM dashboards.

By separating content retrieval from formatting and lead capture, the platform ensures that administrative information is always accurate and updated, protecting the brand reputation of the coaching academy.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Tvira Receptionist avoid making up incorrect information?

Unlike generic chatbots that guess answers, Tvira Receptionist (Ojas.ai) is strictly constrained by a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) framework. It can only generate answers using the verified source documents uploaded to its knowledge base. If an inquiry falls outside the scope of these documents, the system politely flags that it cannot answer and offers to route the request to a human admissions counselor.

Is student data stored securely?

Yes, data security and compliance are built into the core architecture of Tvira Receptionist. All parent and student contact records, chat histories, and personal identifiers are encrypted in transit and at rest. The system conforms to modern data privacy standards and supports integrations with private, secure database clusters.

How long does it take to deploy Tvira Receptionist for a coaching centre?

A standard deployment takes between one to two weeks. This timeline includes uploading and parsing the institute's brochures, training the RAG model on custom policies, configuring WhatsApp and web chat interfaces, and testing the CRM webhooks to ensure flawless lead routing.

Can the receptionist handle multiple languages?

Yes, Tvira Receptionist is natively multilingual. It can detect the language used by the parent or student (such as English, Hindi, Spanish, or regional dialects) and converse fluently, ensuring that language barriers do not hinder the enrollment process.

How does the system transition conversations to human staff?

Whenever a prospect asks a highly specific question not covered in the knowledge base, requests human assistance, or completes the lead-qualification loop, Tvira Receptionist immediately triggers a notification. The complete chat transcript is compiled and sent to the admissions desk via email, SMS, or CRM alert, allowing a team member to take over smoothly.
